Exploring Access Points: Urban Vegetable Sourcing in Sri Lanka's Urban Landscape

  1. Municipal Markets:
    • Urban Baskets partners with local municipal markets in Sri Lanka to source fresh vegetables directly from farmers and vendors. By leveraging these established markets, Urban Baskets ensures a diverse selection of high-quality produce for its customers.

  2. Mobile Vegetable Vendors:
    • Urban Baskets collaborates with mobile vegetable vendors across urban areas in Sri Lanka, providing them with a platform to reach a wider customer base. Through this partnership, Urban Baskets delivers fresh vegetables conveniently to customers' doorsteps, reinforcing its commitment to accessibility and convenience.

  3. Community Vegetable Gardens:
    • Urban Baskets supports community vegetable gardens and urban farming initiatives in Sri Lanka by sourcing a portion of its produce from these sustainable sources. By promoting community engagement and sustainable practices, Urban Baskets aligns with the values of urban residents who prioritize locally sourced and environmentally friendly products.
